My favorite type of music

I love listening to music no matter where I am. I love listening to Cantonese pop music (Cantopop), Mandarin popular music (Mandopop), and English songs.

Cantonese Pop Music
This is a genre of pop music written in Chinese and sung in Cantonese. Most of the songs sung by Hong Kong singers are Cantonese pop music.
Mandarin Popular Music
This is a genre of pop music sung in Mandarin. It originated in Shanghai and it is popular in mainland China, Hong Kong, and Taiwan.
