Story of Playing Piano

I started learning piano in first grade until I got my eighth-grade piano certificate. I enjoyed playing the piano when I am free. It is relaxing and fun to learn a new piece of music until I can play it fluently and move on to the next piece.

This is the piano that I played since I started playing the piano. Even though it does not work very well after years of usage, I continue to use it to play many songs. This piano accompany me for my entire childhood and it means a lot to me.
